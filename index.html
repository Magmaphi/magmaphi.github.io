Lockheed Martin is a technology company, but not a company like google or apple. They invest in aerospace, defence, security, and other advanced technologies. Their mission statement is “We solve complex challenges, advance scientific discovery and deliver innovative solutions to help our customers keep people safe”(http://www.lockheedmartin.ca/us/who-we-are.html), their values are to do what’s right, to respect others, and to perform with excellence. They’ve worked on projects from the hubble space telescope to unmanned aircraft.
