Lockheed Martin is a technology company, they invest in aerospace, defence, security, and other advanced technologies. They’ve worked on projects from the hubble space telescope to laser weapons. Their mission statement is to solve complex challenges, advance scientific discovery and deliver innovative solutions to help their customers keep people safe. They hold three values above all else, these are to do what’s right, to respect others, and to perform with excellence.

History of Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin were originally two different companies; Lockheed, and Glenn L. Martin Company. Both companies were founded in 1912 and both made aircraft. Lockheed was founded by Allan and Malcolm Lockheed, although at the time it was called Alco Hydro-Aeroplane Company and was renamed to Lockheed. Glenn L. Martin Company was founded by Glenn L. Martin. Glenn L. Martin merged with American-Marietta (a mining company) in 1961 to form Martin Marietta. In 1995 Martin Marietta and Lockheed merged to form Lockheed Martin. Lockheed Martin also owns Sandia Corporation which operates Sandia National Laboratories (which is technically owned by the United States’ government) who do research in to nuclear systems, national security, and energy. Lockheed Martin also owns Sikorsky Aircraft who specialize in designing helicopters.
